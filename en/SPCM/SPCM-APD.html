Single Photon Counting Module

SPCM-APD

HOLITA’ newly improved SPCM-APD series Single Photon Counting Module APD operated in the Geiger mode, are used to detect single photos over the wavelength range of 350nm to 1060nm. The SPCM-S-APD series has been developed to offer a unique combination of high quantum efficiency, wide dynamic range and ease of use for photon counting applications

Key Feature

  1. Low dark count rates
  2. Peek PDE： >50% @410; >60% @532; >60% @830
  3. >32MC/S dynamic range
  4. Gate input
  5. Single +12V DC supply

Application

  1. LIDAR
  2. Particle sizing
  3. Optical range finding
  4. Fluorescence analysis
  5. Astronomy abservation
  6. Quantum Cryptography
  7. Ultra-sensitive fluorescence
  8. Photon correlation spectroscopy
